What you can ask

Once connected, talk to your AI client in plain English and it picks the right MCP tool behind the scenes. Concrete examples that work today:

  • "What are the trending Solana tokens in the last 24 hours by trade volume?"

    uses trending_tokens

  • "Show me OHLCV candles for WETH/USDC on Uniswap v3 for the past 7 days."

    uses pair_ohlcv

  • "Who are the most profitable traders in the last month, and what are they holding now?"

    combines profitable_traders_by_token + trader_positions

  • "Trace where the funds from this wallet went and stop at the first exchange."

    runs money_flow (ETH, Polygon, Arbitrum, Base, OP, Tron, BTC, SOL)

  • "Screen this address for mixer / sanctioned / scam exposure and give a risk verdict."

    runs risk_screen

Ready-Made Prompts

Beyond individual tools, the server ships guided workflows that chain several tools and return charts, tables, or diagrams.

money_flow Trace funds across hops, label counterparties, stop at exchanges → diagram + edge table.
risk_screen AML/compliance screen (mixer/sanctioned/darknet) → LOW/MED/HIGH verdict + evidence.
token_dashboard 360° view of one token: price chart + supply + top traders + scam check.
trader_dashboard One wallet: profile stats + holdings / PnL positions + activity time-series.
arbitrage_scan One asset across chains → price-spread % table + bar chart.
