ML Clustering & AML Attribution

NEMESIS applies Machine Learning (DBSCAN) to transaction ledgers via the `AutoClusterEngine`.

  • DBSCAN: Identifies Sybil networks and connected wallet clusters.

The `EntityAttributionEngine` compares behaviors against known OFAC/Sanctioned actors (e.g., Lazarus Group, LockBit) and flags identical inflow patterns indicative of Ransomware.
